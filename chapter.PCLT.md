          Only font vendors should create fonts with this bit zeroed.
          The 7 next most significant bits are assigned by

          vendors. The least significant 24 bits are assigned by the
          vendor. Font vendors should attempt to insure that each of
          their fonts are marked with unique values.</p><div class="informaltable"><table role="" class="informaltable" border="1"><colgroup><col width="3cm"/><col width="3cm"/></colgroup><thead><tr><th role="">Code</th><th role="">Vendor</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td role="">A</td><td role="">Adobe Systems</td></tr><tr><td role="">B</td><td role="">Bitstream Inc.</td></tr><tr><td role="">C</td><td role="">Agfa Corporation</td></tr><tr><td role="">H</td><td role="">Bigelow &amp; Holmes</td></tr><tr><td role="">L</td><td role="">Linotype Company</td></tr><tr><td role="">M</td><td role="">Monotype Typography Ltd.</td></tr></tbody></table></div><h4><a name="idm80794183424"></a>Pitch</h4><p role="">The width of the space in FUnits (FUnits are described

          field.</p><h4><a name="idm80794181776"></a>xHeight</h4><p role="">The height of the optical line describing the height of

          measured height of the lowercase x.</p><h4><a name="idm80794180816"></a>Style</h4><p role="">The most significant 6 bits are reserved. The 5 next
